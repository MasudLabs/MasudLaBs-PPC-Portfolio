# Campaign Structure Templates

**MasudLaBs PPC Portfolio - Tool 5**

Ready-to-use campaign structures for different Amazon advertising goals.

---

## 1. Brand Defense Campaign

**Goal**: Protect branded searches and capture high-intent branded traffic.

### Structure
- **Campaign Type**: Sponsored Products (or Sponsored Brands)
- **Targeting**: Exact match on brand + variations
- **Match Types**: Exact + Phrase
- **Budget**: 15-25% of total ad budget
- **Bidding**: Dynamic bids (up and down) or Fixed

### Recommended Ad Groups
| Ad Group | Keywords | Match Type | Purpose |
|----------|----------|------------|---------|
| Brand Core | brand name, brandname | Exact | Capture direct brand searches |
| Brand Variations | brand + product, brand official | Phrase/Exact | Capture slight variations |
| Brand + Category | brand earbuds, brand headphones | Phrase | Defensive + discovery |

**Negative Keywords**: Add competitor brand names as negative exact.

---

## 2. Category Targeting (Broad + Phrase)

**Goal**: Discover new customers searching in your category.

### Structure
- **Campaign Type**: Sponsored Products
- **Targeting**: Broad + Phrase on category terms
- **Budget**: 30-40% of total budget
- **Bidding**: Dynamic bids (down only) recommended

### Recommended Structure
- Main Category Broad
- Sub-category Phrase
- Long-tail Broad (for volume)

**Negative Keywords Strategy**:
- Add negative keywords aggressively in first 7-14 days
- Use Search Term Report Analyzer tool

---

## 3. Competitor Conquesting

**Goal**: Steal market share from competitors.

### Structure
- **Campaign Type**: Sponsored Products
- **Targeting**: Competitor brand names + model numbers
- **Match Types**: Exact + Phrase
- **Budget**: 15-25%
- **Bidding**: Aggressive Dynamic bids

**Important**: Monitor ACoS closely. These campaigns often have higher CPC but can be very profitable with good creative/offer.

---

## 4. Long-Tail Harvesting Campaign

**Goal**: Capture high-converting, low-competition long-tail searches.

### Structure
- **Campaign Type**: Sponsored Products
- **Targeting**: Broad match with heavy negative keyword list
- **Budget**: 10-20%
- **Bidding**: Dynamic bids (up and down)

**Key Success Factor**: Extremely clean negative keyword list from day 1.

---

## 5. Seasonal / Event Campaign

**Goal**: Capitalize on holidays, sales events (Prime Day, Black Friday, etc.).

### Structure
- Create separate campaign 2-3 weeks before event
- Use broad + phrase targeting on event-related terms
- Increase budget significantly during event
- Pause or reduce budget after event

---

## General Best Practices

| Principle | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Campaign Separation** | Keep Branded, Category, and Competitor in separate campaigns |
| **Budget Allocation** | Start with 70% Category + 20% Branded + 10% Testing |
| **Negative Keywords** | Use shared negative keyword lists at campaign level |
| **Search Term Monitoring** | Review Search Term Reports every 2-3 days in first 2 weeks |
| **Bid Strategy** | Use Rule-based or Dynamic bids for most campaigns |

---

## Quick Start Recommendation (New Account)

1. **Week 1-2**: Launch Brand Defense + one Category Broad campaign
2. **Week 3+**: Add Long-tail harvesting campaign
3. **Ongoing**: Use Search Term Analyzer to clean and expand
